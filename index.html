ABOUT DERRICK

Derrick runs the Derrick Delivery. His online portfolio site features his photography, event emcee and content creation. Derrick was born in Kenya and is an art enthusiast and all time poet. He is inspired by travel, nature, and architecture and likes to create art which has patterns, light and shadows and compositions. His site displays the work across categories in a full-width banner. Following a subtle palette, his images are stunning and make the viewer eager to explore the nature.
